Fan site for the NES Metroid game. (and maybe Super Metroid later)

Games

I've only played through the first 3 games, and then recently the latest (main) game.

Metroid
Good game. For years its password system haunted me, since I had been totally unable to crack it -- but no more! :)
Metroid II
Good times in the car on Gameboy as a child, but I think I would be disappointed if I played this again.
Super Metroid
Great game, the second game (after The Legend of Zelda) which inspired me to create a game-map.
Metroid Dread
Great game in many ways, but I have issues with it genre-breaking my expectations. To be fair, I skipped over decades of changes in the industry/series, but it seems to me the developers decision to emphasize speed-running and "dread mode" over classic metroid-vania exploration was a poor one.
